# GymFlow Roadmap

## Phase 1: Foundation

- Next.js App Router project structure.
- Tailwind design system, light/dark mode, product shell.
- Supabase clients and environment contract.
- Auth routes, protected layouts, role helpers.
- Database migration v1 with core tables and RLS scaffolding.

## Phase 2: Operations Core

- Dashboard with revenue, attendance, due dates, birthdays, AI insight cards.
- Members CRUD with profile, photo, plan, status, notes, injuries, goals.
- Plans CRUD.
- Payments ledger, partial/full payment states, receipt generation.
- QR access control and attendance history.

## Phase 3: Training Experience

- Routine builder for trainers.
- Client routine view.
- Progress metrics, measurements, photos, charts.
- Trainer assignment and client segmentation.

## Phase 4: Business Modules

- Inventory, sales, low-stock alerts.
- Reports: revenue, retention, inactive members, attendance, inventory sales.
- PDF and Excel exports.
- Notification engine for dues, birthdays, promotions.

## Phase 5: Commercial Readiness

- AI business advisor using aggregated views.
- Mercado Pago integration.
- WhatsApp Business integration.
- Billing/subscription layer for GymFlow customers.
- Observability, backups, audit review, onboarding checklist.
